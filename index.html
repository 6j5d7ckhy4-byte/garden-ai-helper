Beginner-friendly garden care rules

💧 Water deeply, not constantly

Most plants prefer consistent moisture with time to breathe. Soggy roots can be as stressful as dry roots.

☀️ Match plant to light

Fruit trees and many vegetables usually need strong sun. Shade plants can scorch in intense afternoon light.

🍂 Feed the soil

Compost, mulch, and soil structure often matter more than quick fixes. Healthy soil supports healthier plants.
