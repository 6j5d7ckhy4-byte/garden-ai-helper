# Garden AI Helper

A beginner-friendly public prototype for a garden and plant health helper.

## What it does now

- Lets someone upload or take a plant/garden photo in the browser
- Shows an instant photo preview
- Collects plant type, visible concerns, sun exposure, watering pattern, and notes
- Generates practical demo suggestions with a simple rules-based engine
- Includes clear disclaimers that this is not a certified plant diagnosis
- Works as a static site that can be hosted with GitHub Pages

## Important privacy and safety notes

Garden photos can reveal homes, yards, street views, location clues, people, or personal property. Users should avoid uploading photos they consider private unless they understand where the image is going.

This prototype does not provide certified agricultural, arborist, pesticide, medical, legal, or financial advice. For serious disease, tree hazards, edible-plant safety, pesticide use, or valuable plants, users should confirm with a local nursery, cooperative extension office, arborist, or qualified professional.
